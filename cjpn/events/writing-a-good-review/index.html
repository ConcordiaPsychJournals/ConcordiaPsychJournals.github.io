May 11, 2021 by Concordia Journals

Writing a Good Review

Event date: May 11, 2021

Watch the Recording
See the Slides

This workshop is mandatory for all members of the CJAP & CJPN team, but all are welcome to join! Join CJAP & CJPN to learn about the review process and how to write empirical and science communication reviews. Dr. Uri Shalev (Psychology Department) will be giving the workshop on how to write an empirical review, and Cristina Sanza (Journalsim Department) will be covering how to edit a science communication article. This workshop will be held online.

Thank you to all who participated!
